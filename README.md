# Retail_sales

# Tools

snowflake sql

# Variable Discription

NUMBER_OF_SALES-	Number of sales transactions recorded on that date (integer)

NUMBER_OF_CUSTOMER-Number of distinct customers who made purchases on that date (integer)

PURCHASE_DATE-	The date on which the sales occurred (date)

DAY_NAME-	Name of the day of week for PURCHASE_DATE (categorical: e.g., “Mon”, “Tue”, etc.)

MONTH_NAME-	Name of the month for PURCHASE_DATE (categorical: “Jan”, “Feb”, etc.)

DAY_OF_MONTH-	Day number in the month of the purchase (integer: 1–31)

AGE_BUCKETS-	Age category of customers (e.g., “Youth Adult”, “Adult”) — buckets used for age segmentation

SPEND_BUCKETS-	Spending-level category of purchases (e.g., “Med Spend”, “High Spend”) — categorizes customers by how much they spend

UNITS_SOLD-	Total number of product units sold on that date (integer)

TOTAL_REVENUE-	Total revenue generated from those sales (numeric, in same currency as unit price)

PRODUCT_CATEGORY-	The category of product(s) sold (categorical: e.g., “Beauty”, “Clothing”)

GENDER-	Gender of the customer(s) (categorical: e.g., “Male”, “Female”)
